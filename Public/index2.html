nuDoors Estimator

Directions:
  1. Measure the Height and Width of each section and enter these dimensions along with the Style and desired finish into "Measurements & Styles".
  2. In the "Hinge Count" section, enter the total number of doors of each size range to determine the number of hinges required.
  3. In "Special Features", enter a 1 for each custom paint color desired.
  4. In "Price Setup", input your pricing values for: Price per Door, Price per Drawer, Refinishing Cost per Sq Ft, Price per Lazy Susan, your On Site Measuring charge, and your Door Disposal Cost.
  5. Then press "Calculate" to get your estimated price.
  6. Tip:Follow our playlists for everything from installation guidance to our ordering guide at The nuDoors Official Channel
